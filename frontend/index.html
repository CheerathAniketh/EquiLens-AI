Real User Story
Priya runs an NGO in Pune. She audits scholarship approvals by gender and caste, finds lower-caste girls at 8% approval versus 34% for upper-caste boys, and leaves with a board-ready fairness report.
Why EquiLens matters
AI systems increasingly shape jobs, loans, scholarships, and healthcare access. If they learn from biased history, they can scale harm quietly.
Before deployment, not after harm EquiLens is built to surface risk early enough to act.
Why teams choose EquiLens
Many fairness tools assume the reader can interpret technical outputs. EquiLens keeps the evidence but explains it in language the audience can actually use.
Student, NGO worker, policy maker The explanation changes tone without changing the evidence.
